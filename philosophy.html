Our Philosophy

The image below depicts the harsh reality of fast fashion - a dumping ground for discarded clothing. At Recycle Shop Apparels, we are committed to combating this issue. We believe in the power of recycling and reusing. Our mission is to create high-quality apparels from recycled and reused cotton fabric, contributing to a more sustainable and eco-friendly fashion industry.

The Fabric

We source our cotton fabrics from ethical and sustainable sources. These fabrics are then recycled and reused to create our apparels.

The Process

Our process involves meticulous sorting, cleaning, and reweaving of the cotton fabrics. This process ensures that the quality of our apparels is not compromised.

The Result

The result is a collection of apparels that are not only stylish and comfortable, but also contribute to a more sustainable and eco-friendly fashion industry.
